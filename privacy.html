Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

Your Privacy Matters

This Privacy Policy explains how Needs & Solutions ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your personal information when you use our website, services, or engage in projects with us.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, company details
  • Project Information: Requirements, specifications, business details
  • Payment Information: Billing details, transaction history (processed securely by third-party processors)
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, device information, usage data
  • Communications: Emails, messages, support requests

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

  • Providing and improving our services
  • Communicating about projects and services
  • Processing payments and invoices
  • Sending updates and marketing communications (with consent)
  • Legal compliance and dispute resolution
  • Security and fraud prevention

3. Data Protection and Security

Security Measures

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Secure servers and infrastructure
  • Limited access to personal information
  • Regular security assessments

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Payment processors, hosting providers, analytics services
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly agree to sharing

5. Data Retention

We retain your information:

  • As long as necessary for service provision
  • As required by law or accounting requirements
  • For legitimate business purposes
  • Until you request deletion (subject to legal requirements)

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies:

  • To improve website functionality
  • For analytics and performance monitoring
  • To remember preferences

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 18. We do not knowingly collect children's data.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries with different data protection laws.

11. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. Continued use of services constitutes acceptance of changes.
